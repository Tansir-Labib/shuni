Archiving Redefined

🎙️

Privileged Audio

Uses Shizuku binder links to access Android's VOICE_CALL stream directly. Crystal clear 32kbps Opus audio capturing both speaker and receiver.

📍

Location Maps

Logs exact GPS coordinates when call connects. Interactively view call marker clusters on free OpenStreetMap views inside the app.

🔒

Invisible PIN Lock

Supports face/fingerprint authentication. Custom keypad entry draws absolutely zero visual characters, preventing shoulder-surfing.

☁️

Google Drive sync

Free personal OAuth sync. Databases and call records are zipped and backed up directly onto your personal Google Drive storage.

How To Setup

1

Install Shizuku

Download Shizuku from the Google Play Store (free, open source utility).

2

Pairing & Launch

Enable Wireless Debugging in Android Developer Options. Pair Shizuku, then tap "Start" to launch the privilege service manager.

3

Install Shuni

Download the latest Shuni release APK, install on your Nothing 3a, grant permissions, and approve Shizuku access.
